About

The Entrepreneurship Summit is the Annual Flagship Event of eCell MSIT which includes Conclave, Competitions, Internship and Job Fair. This year, we have come up with theme 'Upgrading to Upstand' which aims to highlight the importance of upgradation of resources and skills to sustain in this challenging world. Guest speakers are from vivid backgrounds that have got priceless knowledge to share through their experiences.

Internship/Job Fair

Finding the right talent as well as finding the best Internship or Job profile is difficult for both companies and applicants. Internship and Job Fair (IJF) brings a very innovative solution to this problem. It provides a platform for companies to hire India’s best talent for different profiles, and a platform for students to get an internship or a placement in these companies.


eCell MSIT is back with its much awaited Internship Drive. This time we have more companies, more profiles, more locations.

18+ Companies

18+ companies on-board including Jio, GetMyUni, Payme, Moodcafe etc.

30+ Job Profiles

Web Developer( Front end, Back end, Full Stack ), Machine Learner, Photography and Video Editing, Sales and Operations, Marketing, HR, Data Analyst, Software Developer and many more.

How to participate?

  1. Register at msit.myways.in
  2. Answer some personality questions
  3. Read instructions on the portal carefully
  4. Make your resume on the portal
  5. Buy a suitable plan
  6. Select and apply for the internships
